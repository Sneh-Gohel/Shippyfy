Frequently Asked Questions

Your Queries Answered

What types of shipping containers do you offer?

+

We offer a wide variety of shipping containers including standard dry containers, refrigerated containers, open-top containers, flat-rack containers, and tank containers. All containers meet international quality standards.

How can I rent a container?

+

Renting a container is a straightforward process. Simply navigate to our 'Rent Containers' section, choose the type and size you need, and fill out the contact form to get started. Our team will reach out promptly to finalize the arrangement.

What are the payment options available?

+

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can select your preferred payment option during the checkout process to ensure a smooth transaction.

Do you provide delivery services?

+

Yes, we offer fast and reliable delivery services for all our containers. Once your order is confirmed, our logistics team will coordinate a suitable delivery time to get your container to you as quickly as possible.

Are your containers customizable?

+

Absolutely! We provide customizable options for our containers to suit your specific business needs. Whether you need shelving, ventilation, or different colors, just let us know, and we'll work with you to create the perfect solution.

What if I have more questions?

+

If you have any additional questions, feel free to reach out through our 'Contact' page. Our dedicated customer service team is here to assist you with any inquiries you may have.
